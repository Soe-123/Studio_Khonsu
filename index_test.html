KHONSU IS THE ANCIENT EGYPTIAN GOD OF THE MOON.

HIS NAME MEANS “TRAVELLER”, RELATING TO THE NIGHTLY TRAVEL OF THE MOON ACROSS THE SKY.

WHO IS KHONSU?

Studio Khonsu have taken influence in their name from this influential Egyptian God.

The word Khonsu can be translated from the ancient Egyptian word Khenes, which means to travel or to cross.

As the moon deity and the main light in the darkness, Khonsu was believed to look after travellers and was often invoked to aid with healing, virility and protection from the elements.

At studio Khonsu we take a holistic approach in making sure our clients are looked after and in safe hands from our first interaction to the end product.
