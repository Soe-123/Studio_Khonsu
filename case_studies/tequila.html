Tequila! The Little Town That Makes the Liquor Giant

CONTENT, PHOTOGRAPHY, VIDEO, PRINT

A photo story produced for Relais & Châteaux to promote their property in Tequila, Mexico.

An exploration of the heady charms of the Mexican city, as well as lifestyle and interior photography of one Relais & Châteaux’s many award winning properties.

A location with so much character and charm, this was a vital element the client wanted us to capture on this assignment. We aimed to tell the story that goes beyond just the property itself, the authentic town and how these complement each other, creating the magic environment we discovered. A truly Mexican experience.
