FINCH HATTONS

Luxury travel in one of the worlds wildest locations

CONTENT, PHOTOGRAPHY, PRINT

A collection of images photographed for the world renowned, luxury safari camp, Finch Hattons. Located in Tsavo National Park, Kenya. Finch Hattons is set in one of the most picturesque locations we have worked in.

With various different mini ecosystems in such short distances, Tsavo National Park and Finch Hattons safari camp offer the most beautiful backdrop for any photo assignment. We were hired to capture the essence of this wonderful place. Discovering its feel and personal touch.
