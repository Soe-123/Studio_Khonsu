Virgin Strive

A Journeys across the Moroccan desert and the Atlas Mountains

CONTENT, PHOTOGRAPHY, VIDEO, PRINT

A series of images following Richard Branson and a team of keen explorers as they adventure through the deserts and mountains of Morocco. Climaxing with the summit of Mt. Toubkal, the highest mountain in the Atlas range.

The Virgin Strive Challenge is a charity event that runs every year. It is done to help raise money for The Big Change charity, helping re-shape the education system for the better. The Strive itself is a great metaphor for change and achievement, participants being faced with physically gruelling challenges, such as climbing mountains and cycling across deserts, as we are in life, in education, the strive shows us how to face and take on life's challenges. This adventure throws up so many epic and emotional moments that we were brought into capture and helped share with the world. Never missing a key moment in the Virgin Strive, we have been invited back year after year to photograph this event.

The Amazon Rainforest, one of the most important ecosystems on earth. As the largest tropical rainforest in the world it plays such an important part in maintaining the planets temperature. Commonly mistaken for the ‘lungs of the Earth’ due to the large amount of oxygen produced, evidence now suggests rainforests don’t have much effect on the world’s oxygen supply due to the decomposition of dead plant matter.

This consumes around the same amount of oxygen as the living plants produce. However these forests still plays a major role in the global ecosystem. They are now referred to as the ‘air conditioners of the Earth’ due to their dark shades absorbing heat from the sun, ultimately playing a key role in regulating global temperature.
